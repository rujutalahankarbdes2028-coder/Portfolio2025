Hello!

I’m a Communication Design student with a passion for storytelling through visuals, motion, and design. I’m fascinated by how design can shape how people feel and interact—whether through playful illustrations, engaging interfaces, or motion that brings ideas to life.

I love projects that blend creativity and curiosity. I’m still learning and experimenting, but I approach every project with excitement and the goal of making something meaningful.

When I’m not pushing pixels, you’ll probably find me in the nature watching the world quietly, observing small details that often spark ideas later.
