Baroque-Inspired Wine Label Design

This project focused on creating a wine label design inspired by the opulence and grandeur of Baroque architecture. The aim was to reinterpret the ornate, royal aesthetics of the Baroque period into a modern, elegant label that would stand out on contemporary shelves while maintaining a sense of historical richness. The challenge was to translate architectural and artistic cues — intricate ornamentation, dynamic compositions, and luxurious detailing — into a cohesive visual language suitable for a product label.

The final design successfully fused historical inspiration with modern aesthetics, resulting in a sophisticated and memorable wine label. The project demonstrated how classical art movements can inform contemporary design, translating cultural heritage into visual storytelling suitable for modern branding.

Role

As the designer and researcher, I led the project from conceptual research to final visual execution. Merging research-based conceptual thinking with technical design execution, ensuring that every decision reflected the design narrative.

Category

Packaging Design / Visual Identity

Project Type

Academic Assignment

Objective

The goal was to develop a label that captured the essence of Baroque artistry — drama, movement, and luxury — while adapting it to the minimal, refined expectations of modern premium wine branding. The design had to balance historical depth with modern sophistication, evoking an emotional connection through texture, form, and color.

Process

The design process began with in-depth visual research into Baroque architecture — focusing on structural rhythm, decorative flourishes, and use of contrast. I examined how light, shadow, and gold detailing were used to create dramatic effects and emotional resonance. From there, I created moodboards and color studies featuring deep, luxurious tones — burgundy, gold, black, and ivory — to mirror the richness of the Baroque palette. The typography took inspiration from classical serif fonts with ornamental detailing, paired with a minimal layout to keep the design elegant and legible.
The label composition features ornamentation reminiscent of Baroque facades, combined with modern geometry to ensure a refined and balanced visual hierarchy. Finishing touches such as embossed textures, metallic accents, and layered patterns were conceptualized to evoke a tactile, premium feel.
