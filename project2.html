Meta Ad creatives of CeeJe

During my internship at Aarna Lifestyle, I worked on digital ad creatives for CeeJe, a fragrance brand known for its minimal yet luxury aesthetic. The project focused on designing high-performing ad creatives for Meta platforms (Instagram and Facebook) to enhance engagement and conversion. My task was to translate the brand’s identity into visuals that not only aligned with its tone but also caught attention instantly in fast-scrolling digital environments.

The final deliverables included a suite of Meta ad creatives (both static and motion). These designs contributed to increased viewer engagement and higher click-through rates, translating aesthetic appeal into measurable marketing results. The creatives were later adapted across other digital touchpoints, reinforcing the brand’s presence and consistency.

Role

As a Graphic Design Intern, I was responsible for conceptualizing, designing, and animating Meta ad creatives under the guidance of the senior designers and marketing team members.

Category

Digital Advertising / Motion & Visual Design

Company

Aarna Lifestyle (Parent Company of CeeJe)

Objective

The goal was to create striking, scroll-stopping ad creatives that communicated the essence of Ceeje’s fragrances — sophistication, confidence, and individuality — while optimizing them for Meta’s performance-driven formats. These visuals were designed to capture attention, tell a story in seconds, and drive users to purchase, bridging aesthetics with marketing impact.

Process

The process began with researching CeeJe’s brand language — its visual tone, audience persona, and competitors in the premium fragrance market. I then explored visual metaphors that represented scent through imagery — fluid textures, color gradients, and minimalist compositions that evoked mood and identity.
Using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I developed static compositions, experimenting with layout, and product focus. In After Effects, I animated these compositions to create motion ads with smooth transitions, enhancing the sensory experience of the fragrance visually. Each creative underwent multiple iterations based on feedback loops from the art director and marketing team, with adjustments made to improve clarity, balance, and storytelling rhythm.
